Data Privacy and Compliance

Attendify is committed to protecting your data. All attendance records, user credentials, and analytics are encrypted using AES-256. We never share personal information with third parties without explicit consent.

Information collected: Email addresses, attendance timestamps, role-based permissions, and aggregated usage analytics.

Your rights: Access, modify, or request deletion of your data at any time via admin panel. Audit logs are retained for 12 months unless configured otherwise.

For full policy, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@attendify.com.
